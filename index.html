Univesal Accesibility

Mindicraft Network is a groundbreaking blockchain platform designed with the core philosophy of inclusivity and empowerment for all. At the heart of Mindicraft Network is a commitment to democratize the power of blockchain technology, making it accessible and beneficial not only to tech-savvy individuals but also to corporations and government entities.

ECOSYSTEMS

EVERYTHING EXCHANGE

One Boarderless Exchange that allows trading of any assets and prediction of future by empowering anyone and everyone to be a marketmaker.

GOVERNMENT FIAT LAUNCH PAD

Our blockchain network's Fiat Launch Pad is a cutting-edge feature designed to empower governments in issuing their own fiat currency as digital assets. This platform provides a robust and secure framework for the digital issuance and management of national currencies, merging the trust and authority of traditional fiat with the innovation and efficiency of blockchain technology.

Chain POS

Chain POS is an advanced Point-of-Sale system integrating a native blockchain token for secure and instantaneous transactions, offering a versatile, user-friendly platform for the future of retail and service industries.

EVERYTHING EXCHANGE

  • FOR individuals
  • For corporates

An Open Platform for Individuals

Equal access to individuals to be marketmaker by eliminating the financial barrier.

Users can access, create and trade positions of any size. Your position will be paired and matched with a counterparty.

Any on chain assets can be used to create a position, allowing access to a diverse market.

Own your assets. Users can keep their assets in their own smart contracts deployed through the Everything Exchange and trade tokens generated from the smart contract.

Automatic settlement. Every position is guaranteed by funds locked in smart contract and automatically transfer funds during settlement.

An Open Platform for Corporates Trading

Each participator can choose the trader and to sign a contract.

Launch your on chain assets by backing it with real life assets for others to trade.

Become an official price feed and result validator on prediction markets.

Build your own trading system through our API.

Become a market maker through providing liquidity.

FIAT Launch Pad

Launch and Management platform for government and official currency.

Native Monetary Tools for managing quantitative easing, interest rate for official fiat tokens. & other digital.

Fiscal Policy Tools for adjusting and implementing tax rates depending on transaction type and account.

Banking system for government launched Fiat, compliance tool for reserve and accounting.

Cybersecurity measures against black swan event.

Interoperability with existing financial system, Fiat launch can be seamlessly used alongside traditional currencies and within existing financial infrastructures.

What is MIND ICO?

MIND ICO is the public offering of the network's native token, which can be used for establishing validator node and becoming part of the Mindicraft Network, processing transactions, initiating Schedule Messages, and creating third party solutions.

What cryptocurrencies can I use to purchase?

Once ICO period is launched, You can purchase MIND Token with Etherum and Solana smart contract. Your tokens will be allocated on the Mindicraft Network with Proof of Purchase from Ethereum and Solana.

How can I participate in the MIND Token sale?

The Token offering address will be announced during the commence of token sale on ethereum and solana, where funds are locked in the smart contract until the end of ICO period.

How do I benefit from the MIND Token?

MIND token is the backbone and building blocks to the Mindicraft network, with which you gain access to all the platforms, tools and the network itself in creating your own blockchain solutions.
